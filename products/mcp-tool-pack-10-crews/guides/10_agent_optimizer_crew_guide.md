# Guide: Agent Optimizer (Meta) Crew

**When to use:** Improving your agent systems, post-mortems, scaling agents.

**Setup:** Describe your current agents/crews + recent run results or problems.

**Customization:** Include your specific metrics (cost, speed, quality).

**Pro Tip:** Run this weekly on your ops dashboard data.

**Example:** "Audit our current research + writing crews and suggest 3 improvements for lower cost/higher quality."
