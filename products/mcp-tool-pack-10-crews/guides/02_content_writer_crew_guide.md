# Guide: Content Writer Crew

**When to use:** Blog posts, sales pages, newsletters, documentation.

**Setup:** Load YAML → provide topic + style guide (brand voice, length, CTA).

**Customization:** Add your brand guidelines in the Writer's backstory. Link to your content style guide.

**Pro Tip:** Pair with Research Crew output as input.

**Example:** "Write a launch post for the Agent Ops Dashboard Notion template targeting indie hackers."
