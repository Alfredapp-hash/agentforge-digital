# Guide: Research Crew

**When to use:** Deep dives, competitive analysis, trend research, due diligence.

**Setup:**
1. Install dependencies.
2. Load the YAML.
3. Provide your research topic as input.
4. Run and review the verified brief.

**Customization:**
- Swap tools (add Perplexity, arXiv, etc.).
- Change model to grok-4.3 for better reasoning.
- Add your domain-specific instructions in the backstory.

**Pro Tip:** Run this crew first, then feed the output into the Content Writer crew for instant articles or reports.

**Example Input:** "Impact of xAI Grok on solo developer productivity in 2026"

**Expected Output:** Structured research brief with sources, contradictions flagged, and confidence scores.
